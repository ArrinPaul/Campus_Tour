# Campus Virtual Tour - Advanced Features Roadmap

## 1. Navigation & Exploration
- **Spatial "Hotspot" Navigation:** Visual markers (arrows/chevrons) within the 3D scene to allow walking between connected nodes naturally.
- **Interactive Campus Map:**
    - Top-down vector map (SVG) overlay.
    - "You are here" indicator with orientation radar.
    - Click-to-teleport functionality for buildings.
- **Quick Jump Sidebar:** Categorized menu (Departments, Hostels, Sports) for rapid navigation.

## 2. Information & Content
- **Rich Points of Interest (POIs):** 
    - Floating icons within the 360Â° scene.
    - Click to open modals with:
        - Text descriptions.
        - Image carousels.
        - Embedded videos.
- **Audio Guides:** Toggleable voiceover narration for specific blocks or labs.

## 3. User Experience & Accessibility
- **Gyroscope Control:** Mobile-first motion controls (move phone to look around).
- **Transitions:** Smooth cross-fade or zoom transitions between scenes.
- **Accessibility Mode:** Keyboard navigation support and screen reader compatibility.

## 4. Conversion & Utility
- **Call-to-Action (CTA) Overlay:** Persistent "Apply Now" or "Book Visit" buttons.
- **Share View:** Button to copy the current exact angle/zoom URL.

## 5. Advanced / "Wow" Factors
- **VR Mode:** Split-screen view for VR headsets.
- **Mini-map Radar:** Real-time rotation syncing on the map.
- **Gamification:** "Scavenger Hunt" mode to find hidden items.
